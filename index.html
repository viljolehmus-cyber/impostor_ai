ImposterAI

A social deduction party game.
One phone · 3 to 12 players.

How to Play

1. Add players

Get 3 to 12 people together. Everyone shares this one phone.

2. Pick a category

Choose a pre-made list — or generate a fresh one with AI on any topic.

3. See your secret word

Pass the phone around. Each player taps the card to reveal their word. One player is the Imposter — they see no word.

4. Give clues

Each player says ONE word related to the secret. The Imposter must bluff without knowing the word — listen carefully to everyone else first.

5. Vote

Vote for who you think the Imposter is. If the majority catches them — players win. If they fool you — the Imposter wins.
